I'm Truly Sorry

I know words don’t erase the pain I caused, but I need you to know that I’m genuinely sorry for how I treated you. You didn’t deserve the way I acted, the things I said, or the moments where I failed to show you the respect and care you gave me.

I’ve spent a lot of time reflecting, and I see now how my actions hurt you. I take full responsibility for that. There’s no excuse—only regret and a real desire to grow into a better person than I was then.

You were always patient, thoughtful, and stronger than you probably realized. I admired the way you cared, the way you laughed, and the warmth you brought into even the simplest moments. I miss the nights playing games together, joking around, and feeling like everything else could wait.

Mimi, those memories still mean a lot to me, and so do you.

I want you to know something important. I never came back to you when I was down in the dumps or feeling lonely, because I never wanted to use you as a place to land when life was hard. The truth is, it’s when things are going well for me—when life is calm and I should feel complete—that I feel your absence the most. That’s when the pain really hits, because I always imagined you still being there, sharing those good moments with me.

I wanted you to always be here, Mimi.

— Toto
